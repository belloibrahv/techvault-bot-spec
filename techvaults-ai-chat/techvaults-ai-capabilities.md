## 1. Our Core AI Capabilities

We build **intelligence layers** that sit directly inside your existing web systems, WordPress platforms, custom web apps, or mobile interfaces. Our core capabilities cover five key architectural pillars:

### Ⅰ. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) & Semantic Search
* **Domain-Grounded AI**: We train AI assistants to reply using only your private knowledge bases, manuals, documentation, or product catalogs—preventing "hallucinations" and ensuring accuracy.
* **Smart Document Processing**: Ingesting raw PDFs, Markdown files, or database rows and structuring them for instant, semantic retrieval by LLMs.

### Ⅱ. Dynamic Conversational Workflows & Lead Generation
* **Agentic Forms**: Moving beyond static chat text. We build interactive UI components *directly inside conversational threads* (e.g., dynamic lead capture cards, calendars, calculators, and files uploaders).
* **Multi-Turn Form Validation**: Live, inline field validation (e.g., verifying phone formats, emails, and inputs) before submitting data to CRMs.

### Ⅲ. Human-in-the-Loop & Omnichannel Handoffs
* **Platform Bridging**: Connecting AI assistants to active communications channels like **WhatsApp Business API**, SMS (Twilio), Slack, and Email.
* **Intelligent Handoffs**: Automatically routing complex queries, pricing quotes, or frustrated users from AI to live human support agents, carrying over full conversation logs.

### Ⅳ. Multi-LLM Providers & Safety Guardrails
* **API-Agnostic Clients**: Designing custom SDK interfaces that let you switch between Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, or open-source models (like Llama) dynamically.
* **Strict Prompt Injection Resistance**: Implementing request guards, context filtering, and safety layers to block prompt hacking and keep user interactions safe.

### Ⅴ. Event Analytics & Conversion Tracking
* **BI Dashboards**: Building analytics architectures that log bot open rates, message volume, conversion counts (e.g., phone calls, form completions), and unresolved questions.
* **Performance Logs**: Tracking API latency, costs, and response times to continuously optimize prompt design and minimize token usage.

## 4. How We Collaborate to Integrate AI

Our integration process is structured to be seamless and risk-free:

1. **AI Discovery & Mapping**: We study your existing workflows and identify the highest-value areas where AI can save support time or increase sales conversions.
2. **Data & Prompt Design**: We compile and clean the custom data source (knowledge base, FAQs) and write precise instruction sets (system prompts) to anchor the AI's behavior.
3. **POC Prototype**: We build a lightweight mockup of the widget or tool in a staging environment for your team to test and refine.
4. **Full System Integration**: We deploy the widget, connect it to your database and notification systems (CRM, email, WhatsApp), and set up strict error handling.
5. **Analytics & Optimization**: We install tracking events so you can watch conversions and track the immediate ROI of your new AI workflow.
