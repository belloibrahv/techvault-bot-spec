### Academy FAQ
**Q1: What courses does Techvaults Academy offer?**
We offer a range of tech programs including Web Development, UI/UX Design, Graphics Design, Prompt Engineering & AI, Cloud Computing, and Tech Fundamentals. Each program is designed to equip you with real-world, job-ready skills.

**Q2: Do I need prior tech or coding experience to enroll?**
Not at all. Our courses cater to both beginners and professionals. Whether you're just starting out or looking to upskill, our instructors guide you every step of the way.

**Q3: Are the classes virtual or physical?**
You can choose either. We offer both virtual and physical learning options so you can learn at your own pace and comfort. Some courses even combine both for a hybrid experience.

**Q4: Do you provide certification after completion?**
Yes. Every student who successfully completes a course receives a recognized certificate that validates your skills and boosts your career profile.

**Q5: How long do the courses take?**
Our courses vary by program—typically between 1 to 6 months depending on the track and learning mode (virtual or physical).

**Q6: Will I get support during and after the training?**
Absolutely. You'll have access to mentorship, career support, and a vibrant community of learners and alumni—even after completing your program.
